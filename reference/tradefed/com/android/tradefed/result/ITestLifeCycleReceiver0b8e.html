Receives event notifications during instrumentation test runs.

Patterned after org.junit.runner.notification.RunListener

The sequence of calls will be:

  • testRunStarted
  • testStarted
  • [testFailed]
  • [testAssumptionFailure]
  • [testIgnored]
  • testEnded
  • ....
  • [testRunFailed]
  • testRunEnded
